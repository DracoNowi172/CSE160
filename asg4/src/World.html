Notes to Grader

I built this code off of my blocky animal code. theres alot of buttons but the main thing to keep in mind is that to turn the point light on, switch spotlight to off. if you want spotlight on press spotlight on and pointlight will turn off. Both lights can be controlled with their respective sliders, just make sure to press light animation off to enable slider control. My blocky animal code still works so if you want pompom to wave his hat at you press animation On and shift click on him to swap between his states. as per usual. The light color can also be changed by sliding around the RGB slider. and the magenta and yellow cubes have their basic functionality like in professors videos. I reccoment turning on the Normals and turning on the animation for magenta and yellow to more easily see the normals changing. Finally we have the visualizer, it will display the light just like in professors videos (Note: using the visualizer with spotlight on is not reccomended as spotlight has a much wider range and affects the whole screen, meaning if the visualizer is on it will cover the screen in green and red. This behaviour is what was requested however.)
