Controls:

WASD for movement, Q to look left, E to look right, You can also use the mouse to look around!

STORY:

The kingdom of Askr is under attack by Emblian forces, You find yourself surrounded, but there is hope! Defeat the 10 enemy swordfighters and fafnir's to break their ranks and repel Emblia! (Note: If you wish to challenge more opponents you may press the Reinforcements Button)
